Unaccompanied Minors

A Data-Driven Visualization

Introduction

Welcome to this professional visualization project that examines the journeys of unaccompanied minors traveling to the United States. This project highlights data-driven insights into arrival demographics, detainment patterns, and release processes to provide clarity and understanding.

Arrival

This section delves into the demographics of unaccompanied minors arriving in the United States. Key focus areas include:

  • Countries of origin
  • Age and gender distribution
  • Migration trends over time

Detainment

Many unaccompanied minors experience detainment upon arrival. This section visualizes:

  • Average duration of detainment
  • Trends in detainment locations
  • Demographics of those detained

Release

After detainment, unaccompanied minors are often released to sponsors or organizations. Key data includes:

  • Geographic destinations
  • Types of sponsors (e.g., family, shelters)
  • Temporal patterns of release
